1 Accord

Rakesh Lode [Founder]

18 August 2024

1ACCORD is a comprehensive engineering company that offers a wide range of services to support product development and engineering projects. Our expertise spans various industries, and we provide end-to-end solutions from concept design to final implementation. For idea validation, we conduct thorough market research, analyze customer needs, and assess the competition to ensure the viability and potential success of an idea. We also validate technical feasibility, identify necessary resources, and evaluate financial aspects to provide a comprehensive assessment. In concept design and MVP development, we utilize user research, ideation, prototyping, usability testing, and iteration to create innovative and customer-centric solutions. We also focus on visual design and accessibility to ensure a compelling and inclusive user experience.

Our capabilities extend to articulated bot design, SCADA and gantry systems, and FEA and simulation. We excel in designing and optimizing industrial automation solutions, monitoring and controlling large-scale processes, and analyzing complex engineering systems for structural, thermal, fluid dynamics, and other analyses. With expertise in embedded systems and PLC automation, we enable efficient control and automation in various industries, enhancing productivity, safety, and quality. Our proficiency in computer vision and robotics empowers machines to interpret and respond to visual data, driving advancements in diverse applications. Quality control is a key focus, and we provide comprehensive engineering project management, consulting, and ongoing maintenance services. Our manufacturing capabilities encompass printing, packaging, metal fabrication, injection molding, CNC machining, sheet metal, 3D printing, and more. At 1ACCORD, we combine technical expertise with innovation, ensuring exceptional solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients.
