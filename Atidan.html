Atidan Technology

Kartavya Chitalia [Founder]

18 August 2024

Atidan aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of knowledge workers across organizations by leveraging cutting-edge technologies. The company’s mission is to streamline work processes and decision-making by ensuring that individuals have timely access to the right information. This approach helps organizations make well-informed decisions quickly, thereby optimizing their operational efficiency. Atidan holds the prestigious status of a Microsoft Gold Partner, reflecting its deep expertise and strong relationship with Microsoft. This designation highlights Atidan’s proficiency in delivering solutions using Microsoft technologies. Additionally, Atidan's work has garnered recognition from major industry players such as Gartner, Microsoft, IBM, and Apple. These accolades signify Atidan’s commitment to excellence and its influential role in the tech industry.

Atidan provides solutions that leverage AI to drive innovation and efficiency. This includes implementing AI-driven tools and technologies to enhance business processes and decision-making. The company delivers expertise in deploying and managing Office 365, ensuring organizations can fully utilize Microsoft’s suite of productivity tools. Atidan offers comprehensive analytics services to help organizations extract valuable insights from their data, enabling data-driven decision-making. The company assists businesses in transitioning to cloud-based environments, facilitating smoother and more efficient migrations to the cloud. Atidan provides high-quality IT staffing solutions, connecting organizations with skilled professionals to meet their technical needs. Atidan serves a broad range of clients, including several Fortune 1000 companies. Its global clientele spans across the USA and other international markets, showcasing the company’s ability to deliver top-notch solutions to leading organizations worldwide.
