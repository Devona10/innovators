Introduction: Founded in 1984 under the aegis of the Agnel Ashram Fathers, Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (FRCRCE) has grown to become one of Mumbai’s premier engineering institutions. Affiliated with the University of Mumbai and accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), FRCRCE is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

Academic Programs: FRCRCE offers a diverse array of engineering programs at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. The institution provides Bachelor of Technology (BTech) degrees in various engineering disciplines, as well as Master of Technology (MTech) degrees and Doctoral (PhD) programs. These programs are designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of their chosen fields, blending theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

Modern Campus and Facilities: The college boasts a state-of-the-art campus with modern infrastructure. Facilities include advanced laboratories equipped with the latest technology, expansive libraries with extensive collections, and well-maintained sports amenities. This environment supports both academic learning and personal growth, ensuring that students have access to resources that enhance their educational experience.

Faculty Excellence: FRCRCE takes pride in its distinguished faculty, composed of highly qualified professionals and academics who are experts in their respective fields. The faculty members are not only dedicated educators but also mentors who guide students through their academic and career aspirations. Their expertise and commitment significantly contribute to the institution’s reputation for high-quality education.

Placement and Career Opportunities: The college is well-regarded for its robust placement cell, which plays a crucial role in securing internships and job placements for its graduates. FRCRCE’s placement record is impressive, with the highest salary package reaching INR 17 LPA and the average package standing at INR 8.2 LPA. The college’s strong ties with industry leaders and prominent companies ensure that students have access to valuable career opportunities. Notable recruiters include JP Morgan, Siemens, Reliance, Amazon, Accenture, and many more.
